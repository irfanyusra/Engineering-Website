		<h id="subH"><b>What is K-maps?</b></h>
		<p id="one">A Karnaugh map (K-map) is a pictorial method used to minimize
      Boolean expressions without having to use Boolean algebra theorems and
      equation manipulations. A K-map can be thought of as a special version of
      a truth table. Using a K-map, expressions with two to four variables are
      easily minimized.</p>
