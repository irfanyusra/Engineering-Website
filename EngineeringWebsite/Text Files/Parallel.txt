		<h id="subH"><b>What is Parallel circuits?</b></h>
		<p id="one">A parallel circuit has two or more paths for current to flow
			through. Voltage is the same across each component of the parallel circuit.
			The sum of the currents through each path is equal to the total current
			that flows from the source.</p>
