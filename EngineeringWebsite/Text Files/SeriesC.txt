		<h id="subH"><b>What is Series circuits?</b></h>
		<p id="one">A series circuit is a circuit where there is only one path
			from the source through all of the loads and back to the source. This
			means that all of the current in the circuit must flow through all of
			the loads. One example of a series circuit is a string of old Christmas
			lights.</p>
