BENEFITS


This course helps students decide if computer engineering is their future career path, as this course covers most of the basics of computer engineering, both software and hardware. Students make circuits, analyze them and do simulations for to make it more realistic. This course teaches students three most important types of programming languages like Python, HTML and CSS. Students make a website at the end of the course as their summative, which gives them the feeling of the software part of the computer. For the hardware part, students learn circuits both basic circuits and digital circuits. Students also code a simple robot, and do a presentation on it. This course teaches a small part of what students will learn in college or universities in the future.
